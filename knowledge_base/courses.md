# Course Registration and Academic Information

## Course Registration Process
1. Login to the student portal after fee payment confirmation
2. Go to "Course Registration" section
3. Select your semester and branch
4. Choose elective subjects (if applicable)
5. Confirm your course selection before the deadline
6. Download your timetable after registration is confirmed

## Academic Calendar
- **Odd Semester**: July to December
- **Even Semester**: January to June
- **Mid-Semester Exams**: 8th week of semester
- **End-Semester Exams**: 16th week of semester
- **Project Submission**: Last week before exams

## First Year Courses (Common for All Branches)
- Engineering Mathematics I & II
- Engineering Physics
- Engineering Chemistry
- Basic Electrical Engineering
- Engineering Mechanics
- Engineering Drawing
- C Programming
- Communication Skills

## Elective Selection (3rd Year Onwards)
- Electives are offered based on department
- Each student selects 2 open electives and 1 department elective per semester
- Elective choices are on a first-come-first-served basis
- Speak to your department head for guidance on elective selection

## Timetable
- Classes run Monday to Saturday (first year)
- Lecture hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
- Each lecture slot: 1 hour
- Lab sessions: 2-hour blocks
- Timetable is released 1 week before semester start
- Available in the Academic section of the student portal

## Grading System
- **O (Outstanding)**: 10 points
- **A+ (Excellent)**: 9 points
- **A (Very Good)**: 8 points
- **B+ (Good)**: 7 points
- **B (Above Average)**: 6 points
- **C (Average)**: 5 points
- **F (Fail)**: 0 points
- Minimum CGPA required: 4.0 to pass

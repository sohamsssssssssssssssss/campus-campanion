# TCET General Information and FAQs

## About TCET
Thakur College of Engineering & Technology (TCET) is located in Kandivali East, Mumbai.
It is affiliated to the University of Mumbai and approved by AICTE.
TCET offers undergraduate (B.E.) and postgraduate (M.E.) programs in multiple engineering branches.
The college is NAAC accredited with 'A' grade.

## Campus Facilities
- Central Library with 50,000+ books and digital resources
- High-speed WiFi across campus (100 Mbps)
- Air-conditioned computer labs with latest hardware
- Sports complex with cricket ground, basketball court, and indoor facilities
- Auditorium seating 800+ for events and seminars
- Food court with 5 food stalls and a cafeteria
- Medical room with first-aid and nurse on duty
- Parking facility for students (two-wheelers only)

## Anti-Ragging Policy
TCET has a strict zero-tolerance policy against ragging.
Report any incident to the Anti-Ragging Committee or call the helpline.
Ragging is a punishable offense under Indian law.
Anonymous grievances can be submitted through the student portal.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I reset my student portal password?
Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password", enter your registered email, and follow the reset link.

### Can I change my branch after admission?
Branch change is possible after the first year based on CGPA and seat availability. Apply through the academic office.

### What is the attendance requirement?
Minimum 75% attendance is required in each subject to be eligible for end-semester exams.

### How do I apply for a bonafide certificate?
Apply online through the student portal under "Certificates" section. It takes 3-5 working days.

### Where can I find past year question papers?
Available in the college library portal and also shared by department WhatsApp groups.

# Fee Structure and Payment Information

## Tuition Fee Structure (Per Year)
- **Computer Engineering**: ₹1,27,000 per year
- **Information Technology**: ₹1,27,000 per year
- **Electronics & Telecom**: ₹1,12,000 per year
- **Mechanical Engineering**: ₹1,02,000 per year
- **Civil Engineering**: ₹97,000 per year
- **AI & Data Science**: ₹1,35,000 per year

## Additional Fees
- **Development Fee**: ₹15,000 per year
- **Exam Fee**: ₹2,500 per semester
- **Library Fee**: ₹3,000 per year
- **Laboratory Fee**: ₹5,000 per year
- **Student Activity Fee**: ₹2,000 per year

## Fee Payment Deadlines
- **First Semester**: Within 15 days of admission confirmation
- **Subsequent Semesters**: Before the start of each semester (usually July and January)
- **Late Payment Penalty**: ₹100 per day after deadline

## Payment Methods
- **Online Payment**: Through the college portal via Razorpay (Credit/Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking)
- **Bank Challan**: Download from portal, pay at designated bank branches
- **Demand Draft**: In favor of "Thakur College of Engineering & Technology" payable at Mumbai

## Scholarships Available
- **Merit Scholarship**: Top 10% students get 25% tuition fee waiver
- **EWS Scholarship**: For Economically Weaker Section students
- **Government Freeship**: For SC/ST/OBC/NT/SBC students (based on income criteria)
- **Sports Scholarship**: For state/national level sports achievers

## Refund Policy
- Before classes start: 90% refund (minus processing fee)
- Within 30 days of classes: 70% refund
- After 30 days: No refund applicable

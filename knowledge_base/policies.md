# TCET Policies and Regulations

## Attendance Policy
- Minimum 75% attendance required in each subject
- Below 75%: Student is detained (not eligible for end-semester exam)
- Medical leave: Submit medical certificate within 3 days of returning
- Attendance is tracked biometrically and through classroom marking
- Parents/guardians are notified if attendance drops below 65%

## Academic Integrity
- Plagiarism in assignments or projects is a serious offense
- First offense: Warning and re-submission
- Second offense: Grade penalty (one grade reduction)
- Third offense: Fail in the subject and disciplinary action
- Use Turnitin for checking plagiarism before submission
- Group projects must clearly mention individual contributions

## Examination Rules
- Carry college ID card to every exam
- No electronic devices allowed in exam hall (phones, smartwatches)
- Late entry: Up to 30 minutes allowed, no extra time given
- Unfair means: Immediate expulsion from exam, subject marked as fail
- Re-examination: Available for failed subjects in the next cycle

## Code of Conduct
- Students must wear college ID at all times on campus
- Dress code: Formal or smart casual (no shorts, sleeveless in labs)
- Smoking and consumption of alcohol on campus is strictly prohibited
- Damage to college property will result in fine and disciplinary action
- Mobile phones must be on silent during lectures and labs

## Grievance Redressal
- Submit grievances through the student portal under "Grievance" section
- Response time: Within 7 working days
- Categories: Academic, Administrative, Hostel, Harassment
- Escalation: Department → Dean → Principal
- Anonymous submissions are accepted for sensitive matters

## Leave and Absence
- Planned absence: Apply through portal at least 3 days in advance
- Medical emergency: Inform class coordinator on the same day
- Long absence (>7 days): Submit application to HOD with supporting documents
- Industrial visits and tech fest participation count as special duty leave

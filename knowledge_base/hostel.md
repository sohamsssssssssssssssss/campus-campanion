# Hostel and Accommodation Information

## Hostel Allocation Process
1. Fill the hostel application form in the student portal
2. Submit required documents (Aadhar, admission receipt)
3. Allocation is done based on distance from college and availability
4. Confirmation email sent within 5 working days
5. Pay hostel fees to confirm your room

## Hostel Fees
- **Single Occupancy**: ₹85,000 per year
- **Double Occupancy**: ₹60,000 per year
- **Triple Occupancy**: ₹45,000 per year
- **Mess Fees**: ₹42,000 per year (mandatory for hostel students)
- **Security Deposit**: ₹10,000 (refundable at checkout)

## Hostel Facilities
- 24/7 WiFi connectivity
- Common study room and reading area
- Laundry service (twice a week)
- Hot water supply (6 AM - 9 AM, 6 PM - 10 PM)
- Indoor games room (TT, carrom, chess)
- Gymnasium access
- CCTV surveillance and security guards
- In-house medical room for basic first aid

## Hostel Rules
- Entry/exit timings: 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM
- Visitors allowed only in the common area (10 AM - 6 PM)
- No consumption of alcohol, tobacco, or prohibited substances
- Noise hours: Quiet after 10:30 PM
- Room inspection: Monthly by warden
- Damages to property will be charged to the student

## Roommate Matching
- AI-powered roommate matching based on preferences
- Factors: Sleep schedule, study habits, cleanliness, noise tolerance
- You can fill out the roommate preferences form in the "Roommates" section
- Matched roommates are suggested, final allocation by hostel committee
- Roommate swap requests can be made in the first 2 weeks
